Experienced Faculty

The George Telegraph Training Institute (GTTI) offers Commercial Practice Course for individuals looking for back or front office management course in India. The courses offered cover a wide range of topics that include Professional Executive Secretary, Telephone Operator & Receptionist, Office Management, Front Office Management, and Back Office Management under the guidance of highly experienced faculty members.

On successful completion of any of the courses, recruitment assistance is also provided to the students. The Front Office Management Course is designed to prepare a student to perform and manage the front office activity of an office. The student is well trained to manage the daily procedures of a contemporary office. The Professional Executive Secretaryis designed to provide a direct entry in executive level secretary positions through thorough education in necessary skills. Students trained in back office management will be able to handle various back office work like file maintenance, noting, drafting, FAX and E-mail usage, store management and departmental coordination etc.

The George Telegraph Training Institute (GTTI) combines traditional values with modern technology to ensure quality education at the optimum level. Our association with leading manufacturing companies of India ensures that the training provided by us matches the requirement of the Indian industry.
